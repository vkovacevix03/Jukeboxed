Good Kid

About Good Kid

Good Kid is a Toronto-based indie rock band known for their energetic performances, punchy rhythms, and catchy melodies. Formed in 2015, the band’s sound is influenced by a mix of classic rock, punk, and indie pop, which they blend into a unique style that’s as fun as it is musically compelling.

The band consists of five members:

  • Nick Frosst (vocals)
  • Jon Kereliuk (drums)
  • Michael Kozakov (bass)
  • David Wood (guitar)
  • Jacob Tsafatinos (guitar)

Good Kid rose to popularity with the release of their self-titled debut EP in 2018, which featured standout tracks like "Nomu" and "Witches." The band’s music often incorporates themes from anime and video games, which resonates deeply with their dedicated fanbase. Known for their high-energy live shows, Good Kid has built a reputation for delivering unforgettable performances.

They released their second EP, *Good Kid 2*, in 2020, further establishing themselves as a band to watch. Their music is frequently praised for its tight instrumentation, strong vocal delivery, and infectious hooks. Tracks like "Down With The King" and "No Time To Explain" have solidified their place in the indie music scene.

Good Kid continues to produce and release new music, constantly evolving their sound while staying true to their roots. If you’re a fan of indie rock with a playful twist, Good Kid is definitely a band worth checking out.
